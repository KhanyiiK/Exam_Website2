STATICDATA

This section showcases the static data visualizations associated with my API.

Comparing Asteroid Velocities with Names in a Bar Chart

The bar chart provides a visual representation of asteroid velocities in kilometers per second, accompanied by their corresponding names. The x-axis extends from 0 to the total count of asteroids for the selected date. Each asteroid's name is positioned along the y-axis, while the length of the bar above it represents its velocity. This chart serves as a straightforward means for users to compare the velocities of different asteroids on the selected date, offering a clear and concise overview of the data.

Comparing Asteroid Miss Distances with Names in a Scatter Plot

The scatter plot offers a visual comparison of asteroid miss distances from Earth in kilometers, associating each point with the respective asteroid name. The x-axis spans from 0 to the total count of asteroids for the selected date. Each asteroid's name is aligned on the y-axis, and the vertical position of the data point corresponds to its miss distance. This chart simplifies the task of comparing the miss distances of various asteroids on the chosen date, providing users with an intuitive and informative representation of the data.
